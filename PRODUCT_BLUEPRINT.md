# Mnemo — Product Blueprint & Core Thesis

## Core Positioning & Identity
* **Core Positioning**: Mnemo is the finance network where market opinions become verified track records.
  * *Yahoo Finance* informs. Mnemo qualifies conviction.
  * *TradingView* requires complexity/code. Mnemo lets users test, publish, and prove market ideas without code.
* **The Wedge**: Verified reputation + no-code strategy testing + African/emerging markets intelligence.
* **Visual Direction**: Restrained, elegant, slightly uncanny, coherent—like Anthropic/Claude/Google Antigravity energy fused with financial terminal precision. (No loud gradients, no random orb soup, no bloated marketing page).

---

## The Product Kernel: Four Core Pillars
Do not make Mnemo bigger for the sake of breadth. Make it sharper. Focus only on these four core workflows:

```
┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                              MNEMO KERNEL                              │
├───────────────────┬───────────────────┬────────────────┬───────────────┤
│     RESEARCH      │       PROOF       │    PRACTICE    │   TERMINAL    │
│  "Understand"     │     "Prove"       │    "Practice"  │    "Speed"    │
└───────────────────┴───────────────────┴────────────────┴───────────────┘
```

### 1. Research
Help users understand an asset, market, or region faster than they could alone.
* **Core Features**:
  * AI investment memos
  * Market & news summaries
  * Risk radar
  * Social signals from Mnemo users
  * Emerging markets coverage (BRVM, BVMAC, JSE, NGX, EGX)
* **Outcome**: *"I make better-informed decisions."*

### 2. Proof
Help users build a verified investing reputation.
* **Core Features**:
  * Prediction ledger (entry/exit price, target, timeline, status)
  * Verified track record
  * Profile as a shareable "financial resume"
  * Reputation score (credibility tier with sample-size penalties)
* **Outcome**: *"I can prove I know what I’m doing."*

### 3. Practice
Help users test ideas before risking real money.
* **Core Features**:
  * Paper trading ($100k starting capital)
  * No-code strategy builder
  * Automated strategy backtests
  * Tournaments & challenges
* **Outcome**: *"I lose fake money before I lose real money."*

### 4. Terminal
One powerful expert surface that ties everything together.
* **Core Features**:
  * Command-driven financial cockpit / AI market console
  * Ask AI about markets, generate memos, and inspect signals
  * Run backtests, compare analysts, and view regional data
* **Outcome**: *"I can move faster than a normal investor."*

---

## What To Avoid (Anti-Features)
* ❌ Too many social features / messaging bloat
* ❌ Prediction markets (unless they directly support reputation)
* ❌ Excessive gamification (no coins, gems, loot boxes, or spinning wheels)
* ❌ Random, non-integrated dashboards
* ❌ Too many AI agents / generic chatbots
* ❌ Enterprise/family-office complexity before product-market fit

---

## Paid Subscription Logic
People will pay if Mnemo helps them: save research time, reduce avoidable mistakes, test ideas before committing capital, discover overlooked emerging-market opportunities, and build a credible public track record.

* **Free**: Feed, basic markets, public profile, limited predictions, limited paper trading, basic Copilot.
* **Pro**: Unlimited/expanded AI reports, advanced prediction analytics, full reputation profile, advanced backtests, Mnemo Terminal AI, emerging markets radar, exportable investment memos.
* **Research Desk**: Portfolio reports, region/country risk memos, saved reports, premium Copilot.

---

## Guidelines & Constraints
1. **Disclaimer**: Do not promise guaranteed returns or financial advice. The goal is to increase the probability of making better decisions.
2. **Reputation Terminology**: Use "verified track record" or "platform-calculated reputation"—never use "audited" unless there is a real, external audit.
3. **AI Transparency**: Label bot accounts clearly as `AI Research Bot` to preserve integrity.
4. **Local Focus**: Prioritize emerging markets data alongside global indices.
